The Best Movies According to Alex

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Back to the Future

Back to the Future is a 1985 American science fiction film. A teenager accidentally sent back to 1955 in a time-traveling DeLorean automobile built by his eccentric scientist friend Emmett "Doc" Brown (Lloyd), where he inadvertently prevents his future parents from falling in love – threatening his own existence – and is forced to reconcile them and somehow get back to the future.

Star Wars Saga

A science fiction film about an eternity battle about the good Force and the Dark Force.

Dune

Dune is a American science fiction film in the year 10191.
